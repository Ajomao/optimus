Why Optimus Grind

Optimus Grind was founded upon discovering that often small accounting firms struggle to manage workflows. Through first-hand experience, the founders discovered that delegation of tasks, management and worksheets are done manually which complicates determination and payment of overtime fees to employees.

Optimus Grind seeks to create a professional solution to these problems and help employers manage tasks, workflows and worksheets to avoid prejuidice and enhance professionalism.
